Let's Mock Elections

Let's Mock Elections 2 is a mock elections community, founded on the declining Let's Talk Elections community. It was lanched on the 24th of November, 2019.

December elections

The December 2019 LME2 Elections will be held on December 1st 2019. They will feature elections for the President and Vice-president, all three classes of the Senate, and the House of Representatives.

Ranked list of public officials

The amount of points is calculated as follows:

Points Offices
1000 President
750 Vice-President, President Pro Tempore of the Senate, Chief Justice of the Supreme Court
500 Senator, Secretary of State, Attorney General, Supreme Court Justice, Speaker of the House of Representatives
250 Representative, other cabinet member

The amount of points is then multiplied by the portion of the term served (in days). If the full term is served, it is therefore multiplied by 100%, or 1.

After this, the amount of points is multiplied by the arithmetic difference of the score to the Droop-1 quota for the election held (in case on a runoff, the average of both round is taked in account).

The number in brackets is the number of points from finished terms. If a term is not finished, it is noted with FT (Full Term), counting as 100% until the end of the term, but it might go down, if the person leaves office early.
